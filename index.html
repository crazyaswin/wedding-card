Wedding Celebrations

Eight sacred ceremonies across three glorious days
✦
திருமண நிகழ்ச்சிகள்
🌿
Mehendi
மேஹந்தி
📅 22 August · 4:00 PM – 8:00 PM
Intricate henna artistry adorns the bride's hands and feet, each pattern telling a story of love, prosperity, and new beginnings.
🌼
Nalangu
நலங்கு
📅 22 August · 8:00 PM – 10:00 PM
A joyful evening ceremony where the couple is adorned with turmeric paste, rice, and flowers by family — blessing them with beauty and health.
💛
Haldi
மஞ்சள் நீராட்டு விழா
📅 23 August · 10:00 AM – 12:00 PM
Sacred turmeric paste is applied to the bride and groom separately, purifying and illuminating their skin with an auspicious golden glow.
🪔
Pellikuthuru
பெல்லிகுதுரு
📅 23 August · 12:00 PM – 2:00 PM
The bride is dressed and decorated by close family women in traditional attire, accompanied by songs and rituals of beauty and blessing.
🎶
Sangeet
இசை விழா
📅 23 August · 7:00 PM – 11:00 PM
An evening of music, dance, and merriment as both families come together in celebration — a joyful confluence of culture and rhythm.
🕉️
Kashi Yatra
காசி யாத்திரை
📅 24 August · 7:00 AM – 8:00 AM
The groom symbolically sets off on a pilgrimage to Kashi, only to be lovingly persuaded by the bride's father to return and embrace family life.
💍
Nichayathartham
நிச்சயத்தார்த்தம்
📅 24 August · 8:30 AM – 10:00 AM
The sacred engagement ceremony where both families formally announce the union and exchange gifts, setting the stage for the Kalyanam ahead.
🌸
Kalyanam
கல்யாணம் · The Wedding
📅 24 August · 10:30 AM – 1:00 PM
The sacred Saptapadi — seven vows under the watchful fire of Agni — as two souls become one in the presence of family, gods, and the stars.
